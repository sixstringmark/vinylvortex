Old McKenzie Family Tapes

Here are recordings copied from the old family reel-to-reel tapes. You can play a recording using the flash player on this page.

You can also download a recording by clicking on the image.

These recordings are pretty much "raw" with very little editing, and no noise reduction. Humming, buzzing, and gaps of silence are not uncommon. A few of the recordings don't start sound until a few seconds into the playback.

Enjoy!
