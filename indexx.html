This is the home of the Vinyl Vortex Oldies internet radio show. Shows are divided into parts roughly one hour long. To stream a show part, press the button for the show week then press the "Play" button in the part heading in the contents.

This site features the original Top-40 hits of the Sixties. Each show is focused on the music that was on the charts for a particular week of the year, for all ten years from 1960 through 1969. A show is not a randomized stream of songs, but a simulated Sixties radio broadcast, with vintage radio soundbytes such as jingles, bumpers, and commercials, and with announcer comments. We have a library of over 3,000 songs, so the music is truly "deep track".
