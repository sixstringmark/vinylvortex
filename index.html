The latest update - Seventies Show - August
All of the regular weekly shows now have track times and will support skipping to the next or previous actual song. Note that that action is now done with the conventional and buttons on either side of the play button on the main player at the bottom of the page. And if a show does not yet have track information, it will skip forward or backward 45 seconds each time you click.
This site features the original Top-40 hits of the Sixties. A show is not a randomized stream of songs, but a simulated Sixties radio broadcast, with songs carefully organized into segments, along with vintage radio soundbytes such as jingles, bumpers, and commercials, and with announcer comments. Weekly shows are focused on songs that were on the charts for a particular week of the year, for all ten years from 1960 through 1969. Compilation shows feature all of one category of songs - answer songs, mystery mix, movie music, etc. - and are assembled from segments in the weekly shows. And the specials are, well, anything else. We have a library of over 3,000 songs, so the music is truly "deep track".
